Privacy Policy

This application is designed exclusively for internal use at ION Biosciences to automate order processing.

What Data We Access

  • Emails (for order PDFs)
  • Purchase Order details (extracted using Google Gemini)
  • Customer shipping details (used for FedEx API)
  • Invoice and billing information (used for QuickBooks Online API)

Data Handling

All access and refresh tokens are encrypted at rest on secure Google Cloud Platform servers. No data is shared externally or with third-party services outside of Gmail, FedEx, Google APIs, and QuickBooks Online.

Who Can Access This

Only authorized internal staff from ION Biosciences have access to this automation platform and the Google Sheet where orders are listed.
